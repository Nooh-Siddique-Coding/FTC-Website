About Beta Bionix

Beta Bionix Robotics is our school's competitive robotics club where students in grades 6-8 come together after school to design, build, and program robots. We compete in local and regional robotics competitions while learning valuable STEM skills.

Our club meets three times a week in the school's maker space, where we work on everything from mechanical design and 3D printing to coding and electronics. Whether you're a complete beginner or already know how to code, there's a place for you on our team!

We believe in teamwork, creativity, and perseverance. Everyone contributes their unique skills‚Äîsome members focus on building, others on programming, and some on strategy and documentation. Together, we solve problems and create robots that can accomplish amazing tasks!
